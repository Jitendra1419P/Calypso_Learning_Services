Calypso Learning Services Training Calendar

Calypso classroom training participants experience hands-on classroom training delivered by our Calypso Learning Services’ dedicated staff and members of our Product Management team. Calypso Bank in a Box environments are provided to each participant for demonstrations, configurations, and learning exercises. Classroom training is held each quarter with a published training agenda. Classroom size is limited to max. of 15 participants. Keeping up with changing times due to COVID-19, we transitioned from our traditional classroom trainings to Virtual Instructor Led Training (VILT). The trainings are delivered using WebEx and access to Calypso environment via a cloud based VM for hands-on exercises.

Please see below our training calendar for our upcoming trainings. Please click on the View Details for description of each course and the modules covered in the training.

For registration and enrollments, please email learning@calypso.com
