  • FRONT OFFICE CERTIFICATION

The Front Office Certification is intended to validate front office users in-depth understanding of Calypso’s Front-Office platform and the tools available for day to day front office activities. This Certification validates user’s hands on experience about the processes which are necessary to integrate their Trading, Position/Portfolio, and Risk Management with and in Calypso. It also validates user’s experience around dealing with processes like Market data, Trade Capture, Risk and Live P&L Analysis for cross asset classes.

The Front Office Certification exam is offered across three levels:

  • Associate
  • Professional
  • Expert

Each Calypso Certification is only valid for the current version.The learners will need to re-certify their credentials once a new version of Calypso is released.

To successfully complete the program at any level, the learner needs to earn minimum credits specified for each group (Foundation and Advanced) by successfully passing the relevant Certification exams in that group. The learner has the flexibility to choose the exam topics. To know more about the Certification exam and minimum credit requirements for each level, kindly refer below:
